Search Engine Cycle (Click to Learn)

1. Crawling 🕷

Explanation: Bots (Spiders) travel via links to find new URLs.
Why: If a bot can't find it, it can't rank it.

2. Indexing 📁

Explanation: The engine analyzes content and stores it in a massive database.
Why: Only indexed pages are eligible for search results.

3. Ranking 🏆

Explanation: Algorithms evaluate signals (speed, keywords, links).
Why: The goal is to show the *best* answer first.
